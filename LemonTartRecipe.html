What to do

Tart Crust :

  • Take the butter out from the fridge to let it soften
  • Add icing sugar by batches to mix well together
  • Add egg by 2 times to mix together
  • Add almond powder to mix together
  • Add corn starch with seiving it before mixing together
  • Add plain flour by batches with seiving it to mix well together
  • Roll out the dough to freeze it to make it harden to do the shaping

    [Would be better to roll out the dough with a pair of rulers, for an even thickness. Freeze for at least a few hours to let the dough get harden, in order to do the shaping more easily. It can be freezed for a week before baking.]
  • Brush the tart mould with butter, then place the dough into it.

Lemon Filling :

  • Peel the skin from unwaxed lemon to get the lemon zest
  • Boil lemon zest, lemon juice and caster sugar together at low to medium heat to melt them
  • Beat the egg at a separate bowl then pour in the above lemon juice to mix together. Then place them back to the pan with seiving the mixture, to boil at medium to high heat.
  • Get the mixture out of heat to add butter and mix together

Cream :

  • Take the cream cheese out from the fridge to let it soften
  • Add double cream, and icing sugar with seiving it to mix together

    [Pick low to medium speed of the mixer in order not to let the cream get rough]

Tart :

  • Pre-heat the oven for 3 minutes at 240℃, then bake the tart crust for 20 minutes at 150℃.

    [Bake till the tart crust turn to golden color]
  • Pour in the lemon filling with smoothening the surface
  • Wait for the tart to cool down, then place it into the fridge for 30 minutes at least.
  • Add any decoration on top as you wish
