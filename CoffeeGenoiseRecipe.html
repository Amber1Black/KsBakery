Coffee Genoise

What you need

Egg
Caster Sugar
Honey
Plain Flour

165g
55g
15g
70g

Corn Flour
Milk
Butter
Coffee Powder

15g
20g
8g
4g

6" Round Cake Tin is used

What to do

  • Mix the egg, honey and caster sugar together, then boil them to 40°C.
    [Low heat is essential, otherwise the eggs will be cooked.]
  • Use the mixer to mix for 4 minutes with highest speed
    [Until the mixture is hard enough to draw a pattern without vanishing right away]
  • Then mix with lower speed to turn it into silky texture
    [Around 2 minutes, would feel the batter become softer and more smooth]
  • Add in plain flour and corn flour, with seiving them. Fold the flour in gently.
  • Melt the butter to mix it with milk and coffee powder. Then take a little portion from the above batter to mix together.
  • Then pour the batter back to the main bowl to mix them together gently.
    [Get the cake tin ready with sticking baking paper inside it, so as to take the cake out easily after baking.]
  • Pre-heat the oven at 240°C for 3 minutes, then bake for 33 min at 150°C.
  • After the cake is cooled down, decorate it in the way you wish.
    [The cake has to be completely cooled down before slicing it into layers for decoration, otherwise the cake may collapse when slicing it. Also, the cake has to be cool to add cream on it, in order not to melt the cream.]
