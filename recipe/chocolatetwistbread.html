Chocolate Twist Bread

Category : Bread

Yield : 9 pieces

Total Time : 3 hours

Date Created : 30/08/2024

❮ ❯

What you need

Bread
Flour

360g


Sugar

30g


Salt

2g


Yeast

6g


Milk

250g


Butter

20g

★ Hazelnut Paste : Add according to preference

What to do

  • Mix all ingredients (except butter) together, and then knead it for 3 minutes at low speed. Then the below rough dough would be formed.
  • Add butter then knead it at medium speed for 4 minutes, until a smooth dough is formed.
  • Knead the dough into round shape, then let it rise under a warm condition until its size is doubled. Cover it with wet cloth otherwise the dough will be dried out.
    [Time may vary under different weather condition, in Summer it can be an hour, in Winter it can be 2 hours.]
  • Divide the dough by 9 portion (~72g @), knead them into round shape and let them rest for 15 minutes.
  • Do the formation as below :
  • Let them proof for 30 minutes at least, the size should be 80% more (ideally it's doubled).
    [The last proofing time depends on weather also. In Summer it can be 30 minutes, in Winter it can be 1 hour.]
  • Add almond flake on top before baking.
  • Bake it for 20 minutes, at 180℃.
    [Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
  • After taking out of the oven, brush the bread with butter to make it look shiny.
