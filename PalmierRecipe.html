Butterfly Palmier

What you need

Plain Flour
Strong Flour
Butter
Cold Water
Caster Sugar

150g
50g
150g
60g
40g

What to do

  • Mix the plain flour and strong flour together
  • Chop the butter into pieces, then add to the flour. Mix the flour and butter together by hand softly.
  • Add in water, continue to mix by hand until the below is formed. Then wrap it to put into the fridge to freeze for 30 minutes.
  • Roll the dough into large plain dough, then fold it to 1/3 length and freeze it again for another 30 minutes. Repeat this step for 2 times.
  • Roll the dough out into large plain dough, then add sugar to it. Roll the dough to inside from both sidelines, with one side slightly bigger than the other. Freeze for another 20 minutes.
    [Press the sugar lightly to make it stick to the dough]
  • Cut the dough into pieces to form the butterfly shape
  • Add more sugar on top before baking.
  • Preheat at 240℃ for 3 minutes. Bake for 20 minutes at 160℃.
    [Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
