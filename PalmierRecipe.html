PASTRY

Butterfly Palmier

What you need

Plain
Flour

150g

Strong
Flour

50g


Butter

150g

Cold
Water

60g

Caster
Sugar

40g

What to do

  • Mix the plain flour and strong flour together
  • Chop the butter into pieces, then add to the flour.
    Mix the flour and butter together with your hand.

    [Not necessary to squeeze too hard at this stage]
  • Add water to it, continue to mix with your hand, until the below is formed. Then wrap it to put into the fridge to freeze for 30 minutes.
  • Roll the dough into large plain dough, then fold it to 1/3 length and freeze it again for another 30 minutes. Repeat this step for 2 times.
  • Roll the dough into large plain dough, then add sugar to it. Roll the dough to inside from both sidelines, with one side slightly bigger than the other. Freeze for another 20 minutes.

    [Press the sugar lightly to make it stick to the dough]
  • Cut the dough into pieces to form the butterfly shape
  • Add more sugar on top before baking.
  • Preheat at 240℃ for 3 minutes. Bake for 20 minutes at 160℃.

    [Pick fan option of the oven if available. Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
