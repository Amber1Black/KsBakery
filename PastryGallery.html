Palmier

Palmiers are a classic French pastry, typically shaped like a palm leaf or butterfly. Their irresistible crispy texture makes them hard to resist, as you find yourself reaching for one after another. A sprinkle of sugar on top gives the palmiers a shiny finish, while the sugar nestled between the layers enhances their delightful crunch. Let's try to bake it at home →

Choux Pastry

Choux pastry is wonderfully crunchy when freshly baked. You can fill them with a variety of creams to elevate the flavor and make each bite even more delightful.

Macaron

Macarons are often considered the jewel of French pastries in Europe. They can be pricy to purchase from a store, so why not try making them at home? Although they require some effort to bake, the satisfaction of seeing them rise in the oven is well worth it!
