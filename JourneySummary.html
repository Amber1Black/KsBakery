Kensington Garden

Kensington Gardens is one of the Royal Parks of London. It's located at the west of city center of London, next to Hyde Park.

The Round Pond inside is very beautiful. When there's sunshine, the scene at the Round Pond is stunning.

Earlswood Lake

Earlswood Lake situates in West Midlands, near Solihull, just ~10min ride from the centre of Solihull.

It's a great place for a relaxing walk. There are walking trails surrounding the lakes. Good to go for enjoying weekend / holiday.

Primrose Hill is famous for taking a view of all the famous architecture of Central London, by standing at the top of the central hill.

The Regent's Park is one of the Royal Parks of London. It's a very big park, full of different vibes.
