COOKIE

Mario Mushroom

What you need


Butter

240g

Caster
Sugar

80g


Egg

1


Flour

370g

Color
Gel

Little

What to do

  • Take the butter out from the fridge to let it soften
  • Mix butter with the sugar by batches
  • Add eggs to the mixture by batches
  • Add flour by batches, with seiving it
  • After mixing everything well together, divide the dough into 2 portion and add color gel (red and yellow). Then roll them into large plain dough, and freeze it.
  • When the dough became hard after freezing for few hours (can be overnight or even longer), do the formation as below :
  • Bake for 150 degrees for 15 min
    [Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
  • After the cookie has cooled down, melt the chocolate to add eyes for the mushroom and stars :
  • The mario mushroom and star cookies are ready!
