COOKIE

Flower Cookie

What you need


Butter

245g

Caster
Sugar

95g


Egg

50g

Plain
Flour

290g

What to do

  • Take the butter out of the fridge to let it soften
  • Add sugar by batches to mix well together
  • Add egg by 2 times to mix well together
  • Add flour by batches to mix well together, with seiving the flour.
  • Pipe the batter to flower shape with nozzle 1M or 2D
  • After piping, place the batter into fridge to freeze for an hour before baking.
  • Pre-heat the oven at 240℃ for 3 minutes, then bake for 18 minutes at 180℃.
    [Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best. Bake till the cookie turned golden colour.]
