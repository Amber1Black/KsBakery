COOKIE
Dress Cookie with Chocolate

What you need

Butter

240g

Sugar

160g

Egg

1

Flour

370g

What to do

  • Take the butter out from the fridge to let it soften
  • Mix butter with the sugar by batches
  • Add eggs to the mixture by batches
  • Add flour by batches, with seiving it
  • After mixing everything well together, roll them into large plain dough, and freeze it.
  • Cut them into any shape as you wish
  • Bake for 150 degrees for 15 min
