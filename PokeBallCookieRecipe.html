COOKIE

Pokemon Poke Ball

What you need

Butter

240g

Sugar

80g

Egg

1

Flour

370g

Color
Gel

Little

What to do

  • Take the butter out from the fridge to let it soften
  • Mix butter with the sugar by batches
  • Add eggs to the mixture by batches
  • Add flour by batches, with seiving it
  • After mixing everything well together, divide the dough into 3 portion and add color gel. Then roll them into large plain dough, and freeze it.
  • When the dough became hard after freezing for few hours, do the formation as below :
  • Bake for 150 degrees for 15 min

    [Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
  • Poke Ball is ready to catch pokemon!
