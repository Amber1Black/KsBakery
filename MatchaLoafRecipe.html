BREAD

Matcha Loaf

What you need

Bread
Flour

360g


Sugar

15g


Salt

2g


Yeast

5g

Double
Cream

20g


Milk

30g


Water

210g


Butter

20g

Matcha
Powder

5g

What to do

  • Mix all ingredients (except butter) together, and then knead it.
  • When a rough dough is formed (~kneading for 3 minutes), add butter then knead again.
  • Knead until a round dough with smooth surface is formed
  • Divide the dough into 2, with 1 adding matcha powder into it. Keep kneading and adding matcha powder until it turns green.
    [The adding volume of every type of powder may vary, add until you feel enough with it.]
  • Let it rise under a warm condition until its size is doubled.
    [Time may vary under different weather condition, in Summer it can be an hour, in Winter it can be 2 hours.]
  • Press the dough gently to push out any air inside. Then knead into round dough again and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  • Do the formation as below :
  • Let it proof for 30 minutes at least, the size should be 80% more (ideally it's doubled).
    [The last proofing time depends on weather also. In Summer it can be 30 minutes, in Winter it can be 1 hour.]
  • Brush some milk on top of it before baking, to let it looks better after coming out of the oven.
  • Bake it for 20 minutes, at 180℃.
    [If there's fan option with the oven, please choose it. Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
