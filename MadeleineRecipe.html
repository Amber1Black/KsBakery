CAKE

Madeleine

What you need


Egg

70g

Caster
Sugar

70g


Honey

5g

Vanilla
Extract

3g

Plain
Flour

85g

Baking
Powder

2g


Butter

85g

What to do

  • Mix the egg with caster sugar
  • Add honey and vanilla extract and mix them together
  • Add plain flour and baking powder for mixing, with seiving them
  • Melt the butter into liquid, before adding to the mixture
  • Brush butter to the mould, before filling the batter in.
  • Preheat the oven for 3 minutes at 240℃, bake for 12 minutes at 150℃.
  • Decorate the madeleines in any way you wish to
