BREAD

Pumpkin Bread

What you need

Dough :

Bread
Flour

180g


Sugar

15g


Salt

1g


Yeast

3g


Milk

125g


Butter

10g

Top :


Butter

40g


Egg

16g

Caster
Sugar

25g

Plain
Flour

70g

Almond
Powder

8g

★ 6 Pumpkin Breads can be made

What to do

  • Mix all ingredients for the dough (except butter) together, and then knead it.
  • When a rough dough is formed (~kneading for 3 minutes at low speed), add butter then knead again.
  • Knead until a round dough with smooth surface is formed
  • Let it rise under a warm condition until its size is doubled.
    [Time may vary under different weather condition, in Summer it can be an hour, in Winter it can be 2 hours.]
  • Meanwhile, let's make the pumpkin top.
    Mix all ingredients for the top together, then fridge it for an hour.
    [Mix butter with sugar first, then add egg, flour and almond powder on sequence.]
  • When the mixture gets harder, divide them into 6 portion. Keep them in the fridge until the dough is ready.
  • Back to the dough, when the size became double, put a finger in, a hole can be formed and lasted for a while without shrinking.
  • Press the dough gently to push out any air inside. Then divide the dough into 6 portion, each would be ~60g. Knead them into round dough and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  • Do the formation as below, with placing the pumpkin skin on top :
  • Let it proof for 30 minutes at least, the size should be 80% more (ideally it's doubled).
    [The last proofing time depends on weather also. In Summer it can be 30 minutes, in Winter it can be 1 hour. ]
  • Brush some milk on top of the bread before baking, to let them look better after coming out of the oven.
  • Pre-heat the oven for 3 minutes at 240℃, then bake for 17 minutes, at 180℃.
    [Fan option of the oven would be good to be picked. Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
  • Place a little stick on top, then here comes our Pumpkin Bread.
