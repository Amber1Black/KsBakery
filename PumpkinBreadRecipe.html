What to do

Top :

  • First take butter out of the fridge to let it soften. Then mix with other ingredients by sequence :
    Caster Sugar   →   Egg   →   Plain Flour   →   Almond Powder
  • When everything is mixed well together, fridge it for an hour.
  • When the mixture gets harder, divide them into 12 portion. Keep them in the fridge until the dough is ready.

    • Dough :

  • Mix all ingredients (except butter) together, and then knead it for 3 minutes at low speed. Then a rough dough would be formed.
  • Add butter then knead it at medium speed for 4 minutes, until a smooth dough is formed.
  • Knead the dough into round shape, then let it rise under a warm condition until its size is doubled. Cover it with wet cloth otherwise the dough will be dried out.
    [Time may vary under different weather condition, in Summer it can be an hour, in Winter it can be 2 hours.]
  • Wait for the dough to reach its double size to move to the next step
  • Divide the dough by 12 portion, knead them into round shape and let them rest for 15 minutes.
  • Knead each round dough into round shape again, then wrap them into the "Top" dough as shown in the video at the bottom.
  • Let it proof for 30 minutes at least, the size should be doubled.
    [The last proofing time depends on weather also. In Summer it can be 30 minutes, in Winter it can be 1 hour. ]
  • Pre-heat the oven for 3 minutes at 240℃, then bake for 17 minutes, at 180℃.
    [Every oven are different, have to do some testing to see which duration and temperature suit the best.]
